Alexis Crib with Rose Garland by Newport Cottages

Product Description

Extend the life of your crib (Newport Cottages 4-in-1 Convertible Crib - sold separately) with these Wooden Full Size Bed Rails. Designed to easily convert your crib into a full size bed, the rails replace the sides of the crib, and feature bolt-on assembly for attaching a headboard and footboard (mattress and box spring not included).

  • Expertly crafted by Newport Cottages; Easy assembly for you
  • Strong & sturdy wood construction
  • Converts your crib to a full size bed (headboard and footboard)
  • Accommodates Full size mattress. (Mattress Sold Separately)
  • Assembled dimensions: 55.25 inch width x 76 inch length x 5 inch height
  • Available in White with softly distressed Black top rails
  • JPMA certified to meet all safety standards set by the CPSC & ASTM

Newport Cottages was founded around the idea of making safe, high-quality nursery furniture affordable for all families. That's why all Newport Cottages products are tested above and beyond industry standards.
